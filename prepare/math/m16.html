    Using Pythagorous theorem,

    a² + b² = (b + 8)² = b² + 64 + 16b

    ⇒ a² = 16b + 64 = (4b – 8)² = 16b² + 64 – 64b

    ⇒ 16b² – 80b = 0

    ⇒ b = 0 or 5

    Putting b = 5 in (3),

    a = 4b – 8 = 20 – 8 = 12

    Area of rectangle = 12 × 5 = 60
